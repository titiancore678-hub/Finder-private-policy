Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 13, 2026

At Finder, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Finder mobile application. By using Finder, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information
When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and Google account info (if using Google Sign-In) to create and manage your account.
Freelancer Profile
To power AI matching, we collect your service description, ideal client type, budget range, location preference, and preferred platforms.
Usage Data
We collect data on searches performed, leads saved, outreach messages generated, and lead status updates (Contacted, Replied, Converted).
Device Information
Basic device info such as device type, OS version, and app version for compatibility and performance improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

AI Client Matching
Your profile data is processed by Google Gemini AI to generate matched client profiles tailored to your needs.
Outreach Generation
Your profile details are used to generate personalised outreach DMs and emails for each matched client via the Gemini API.
App Functionality
Saved leads, status updates, and pipeline data are stored so you can track your outreach progress across sessions.
App Improvement
Anonymised usage patterns help us improve the app. We never sell this data.

3. Data Storage & Security

Database
Your data is stored securely using MongoDB within Emergent's secure environment, encrypted at rest and in transit.
Authentication
Login is handled via secure Google OAuth or email/password. We never store your Google password.
Data Retention
Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.
Security Measures
We implement HTTPS encryption, secure API key management, and regular security reviews.

4. Third-Party Services

Google Gemini API
We use Google Gemini AI to power client matching and outreach generation. Your profile data is sent to Google's API. Google's privacy policy applies.
Google Sign-In
If you use Google Sign-In, we only receive your name and email address from Google.
Emergent Platform
Finder is built on Emergent.sh infrastructure. Your data is hosted within their secure cloud environment.
No Data Selling
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing or advertising purposes. Ever.

5. AI-Generated Content

Mock Client Profiles
Client profiles shown in Finder are AI-generated simulations based on your criteria. They do not represent specific real individuals or companies unless explicitly stated.
Outreach Messages
AI-generated outreach messages are suggestions only. You are responsible for reviewing any message before sending it to a potential client.
Accuracy Disclaimer
While our AI strives for accuracy, we cannot guarantee that all generated profiles or messages are perfectly accurate. Always use your judgment.

6. Your Rights & Choices

Access Your Data
You can view all your profile information, saved leads, and account details within the app at any time via the Profile section.
Edit Your Profile
You can update your freelancer profile at any time from the Profile page.
Delete Your Data
You may request complete deletion of your account and all data by emailing us. We process deletion requests within 30 days.
Opt Out
Uninstalling the app stops all future data collection. Contact us to request data deletion after uninstalling.

7. Children's Privacy

Age Requirement
Finder is intended for freelancers and small business owners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
Parental Consent
Users between 13 and 18 years of age should use this app with parental awareness and consent, particularly regarding any future payment features.

8. Changes to This Policy

Policy Updates
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
Continued Use
Your continued use of Finder after changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
